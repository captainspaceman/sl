About Sunland

With roots that go deep into the Orange County and Southern California soil, Sunland Window cleaning and building services is a true family business. Based out of Brea California, Sunland is owned and operated by the Kincer Family, Orange county residents for over four generations. Providing our customers with a clean and functional business is what we have been doing for over two decades, and we take pride in the services that we offer to ensure that commercial structures and office buildings shine.

Anyone who owns or works within a major building or structure is very familiar with the amount of maintenance it takes to keep a building looking good. The punishment that mother nature serves out to the structures that we spend so much of our daily lives in is never ending. Keeping your business clean and well maintained is our number one concern at Sunland. A clean building is a busy building, at Sunland we specialize in making buildings clean, so you can stay busy.
